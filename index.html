How to prepare jollof rice

Jollof rice contain rice as the main ingredient, rice is well known source of fibre. Tomatoes and peppers are also good for health maintenance, They contain lots of vitamin and prevent cancer. Onion are good for you. They reduce inflammation and heal infection.
I derive satisfaction eating jollof rice, it has been my best diet from childhood. To me, this method of preparing rice among others is superior. Apart from the attractive colour, aroma, it gives unique taste if prepared properly.

Needed ingredients

  • 3 cups of parboiled rice
  • 2 cups of Tomato stew
  • Pepper and salt
  • 2 medium onion
  • Chicken stock
  • 3 Knorr cubes
  • Curry and thyme powder

Cooking steps

  1. Pour the chicken and tomato stew into a pot and leave to boil 😉
  2. Add the drained rice, Curry, salt, thyme powder and pepper to taste 😋
  3. Cover the pot and leave to cook on low to mediunm heat 😍
  4. check regularly and widraw fron the heat when its perfectly done 🧐
  5. You can add vegetables and cover it to steam up 😋
